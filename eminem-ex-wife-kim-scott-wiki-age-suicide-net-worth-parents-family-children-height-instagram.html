Eminem Ex-Wife Kim Scott – Eminem’s Ex-Wife Kimberly Anne Scott

Kim Scott is the ex-wife of Eminem. Marshall Mathers and Kim Scott married in 1999 and divorced in 2001. They were briefly remarried for a few months in 2006. In 2010, rumors circulated alleging the two were getting back together until a spokesperson for Eminem made a public clarification about their relationship.

“Contrary to false rumors spread by unreliable tabloids, Marshall and Kim Mathers are not romantically reunited in any way, nor are they expecting a child together,” a rep said at the time. “They are a divorced couple who share custody of their daughter and maintain a friendly relationship.”

Marshall Mathers and Kim Scott have a daughter together, Hailie Jade. Eminem also adopted Kim’s daughter, Whitney Scott Mathers, from a previous relationship, and Kim’s late sister Dawn Scott’s daughter, Alaina Marie Mathers. Dawn Scott died in 2016 at age 41 from a reported heroin overdose.
