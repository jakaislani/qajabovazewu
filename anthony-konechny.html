Career Overview

Anthony Konechny is a talented actor who has been involved in the movie and TV series industry since high school. He started his on-screen journey as Chris in the short horror movie, No Means No.

In 2009, he had a short appearance as a delivery boy character in the action-adventure and drama TV series Smallville. Following year, he again had a small appearance in The Troop TV series. 

He got his first major role in the comedy-drama and romance TV movie Perfect Match, where he portrayed the role of Paul Taylor. Currently, he is working as the cast member of the upcoming comedy romance movie, Married by Mistake.
