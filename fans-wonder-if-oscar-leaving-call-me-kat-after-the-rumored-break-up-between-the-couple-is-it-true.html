A sitcom character from Call Me Kat has gotten speculated to be leaving the show as he is breaking up with the main lead Kat. Continue reading if you want to learn more about it.

Oscar or Kat’s boyfriend is a fictional character portrayed by Christopher Rivas in the American television sitcom Call Me Kat.

Christopher is an American actor from New Your, United States, and an alma mater of California Institute of the Arts who also does storytelling and is making her debut as an author with the book Brown Enough.

Is Oscar Leaving Call Me Kat Following The Break-Up With Kat?

Oscar, one of the American sitcom Call Me Cat characters, based on the British sitcom Miranda by Miranda Hart, broke up with Kat, the sitcom’s main character.

Kat was in a dilemma to choose between her current boyfriend Oscar and longtime love interest Max. In the 28th episode, released on the 14th of April 2022, the main character kissed Max and rejected Oscar’s marriage proposal.

Following this scenario, fans have speculated that he might be leaving the show; however, he recently got promoted to a series regular as per a source, so the writers might have something up their sleeves to develop the character, and he might not be leaving.

Christopher Rivas Wikipedia: The Actor Who Plays Thr Role Of Oscar

Rivas is the actor who has portrayed the role of Oscar in the American television sitcom Call Me Cat, and he is also a storyteller born and raised in New York City, United States.

Christopher launched his career professionally in 2009 while appearing as Basketballapostle in ‘If Not Now When;’ furthermore, he has accumulated 29 credits on his IMDB profile throughout the years.

The actor is famed for making his appearance as Christopher Rivas in Calm Your Curls, Mookie in 2 Broke Girls, and X-Ray Technician in GLOW. Moreover, he is also the voice of Mateo Hernandez in the videogame Call of Duty: Vanguard.

As a storyteller, he has developed storytelling workshops, including the United Nations High Commission for Refugees, The Museum of Broken Relationships, LAMP on Skid Row, UCLA, and CalArts. He also participated in the faculty of The Ariane de Rothschild Fellowship in 2018.

Where Is Christopher Rivas Going After Departing From The Show?

Christopher has not disclosed if he is leaving the show or not yet, and his departure from Call Me Kat is only speculation that is yet to be proven. Moreover, the actor has not revealed his plans to the media yet.

Nonetheless, Rivas recently made his first book Brown Enough available for pre-order, expected to get published on the 13th of September 2022. In his book, he shared the true stories about love, violence, the student loan crisis, race, Hollywood, family, and making it in America. Moreover, he has also gotten engaged in podcasts.
