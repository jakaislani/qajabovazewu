Jason Statham: Career

From an athlete to model, Statham had switched his career from sports to the area of glamour.

He did several advertisements and endorsements for the fashion companies Tommy Hilfiger and French Connection.

His modeling career later paved the way into the movie business when he was hired as an actor in Lock, Stock, and Two Smoking Barrels.

In 2002, he starred in an action movie, The Transporter; his persona changed into an action star.

The muscular physique, bald head, and good looks certainly made him a style icon of that era,

Both men and women instantly loved him all across the world.

He then started appearing in several action movies like The Italian Job, Crank, Death Race, The Expendables, and the Fast and Furious franchise.
