How old is John Corbett?

He was born on 9th May 1961. This places John Corbett age at 58.

Early life

He was born John Joseph Corbett in the city of Wheeling, situated in the state of West Virginia. His parents’ names are Sandra (née Pavilack) and John Marshall Corbett, and he is an only child.

He took up playing the guitar at the age of seven. At the age of 16, he worked as a security guard at the Capitol Music Hall. During this time, he lived with his mother and stepfather.

Education

For his education, he attended the Wheeling Central Catholic High School from where he graduated in 1979. After this, he moved to Bellflower, California where his father and stepmother lived. There, he enrolled in hairdressing classes at Cerritos College, which is situated in the city of Norwalk. At college, he studied hairdressing during the day and took acting classes at night. He is a licensed hairstylist and has been one since 1986.

Career

Acting was not the first path he took. Before enrolling at Cerritos College, he took the test to become a deputy sheriff in Los Angeles County but failed.

When he started taking acting seriously, he landed a Samsung advertisement that later led to him appearing in over fifty commercials that were broadcasted nationally.

He landed his first major role in 1988 after taking part in ABC’s The Wonder Years, which was a comedy-drama series. He was acting under the name Jack Corbett at the time. This was followed by him starring in CBS’s Northern Exposure as Chris Stevens between 1990 and 1995. For this role, he was nominated for the Golden Globe Award for the Best Supporting Actor – Series, Miniseries or Television Film, and the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series.
