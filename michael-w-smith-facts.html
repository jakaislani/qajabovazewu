The Life of Michael W. Smith

Michael W. Smith is a Grammy award-winning Christian singer and songwriter. He was born on October 7, 1957, in Kenova, West Virginia, United States. He grew up in an influential environment, with his father being an oil refinery worker and his mother, a caterer. Michael W. Smith developed a love for music early in life and started playing the piano when he was only five years old.

Career

Michael W. Smith kicked off his music career in 1983 with his first album, “Michael W.
