Coconut Peach Cooler

Summer in a glass! You're going to love the fresh, summer flavors of this refreshing Coconut Peach Cooler!

Course Beverage Cuisine American Keyword coconut peach cooler Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Total Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 2 servings Calories 121kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 1 peach ripe
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • 1/4 cup white rum
  • peach slices and fresh mint sprigs to garnish optional

Instructions

  • Peel, pit and slice the peach.

  • Place peach slices, coconut water and rum in a blender. Pour into 2 ice-filled glasses and garnish with fresh peach slices
