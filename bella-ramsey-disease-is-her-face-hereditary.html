Bella Ramsey Disease- What Disease Does She Have?

English actress Bella Ramsey does not have any concerning diseases. The 19-year-old is doing fine as of January 2023 and is active on social media.

However, in October 2018, Ramsey posted a thread on her Twitter account, revealing to her fans that she was diagnosed with anorexia nervosa.

Anorexia nervosa is an eating disorder characterized by food restriction, low weight, fear of gaining weight, body image disturbance, and overpowering desire to be thin.

Ramsey revealed that she went to counseling sessions trying to pinpoint precisely what was causing her to have an eating problem.

Since then, Ramsey has not posted any updates on the problem, and it seems she has already recovered.

She regularly uploads her pictures on her Instagram, and looking at them, we can ascertain that Ramsey has no illness.

Bella Ramsey Comes Out As Gender Fluid

Bella Ramsey recently came out as a non-binary and said she doesn’t mind pronouns when referring to her.

Ramsey told The New York Times that she is okay if someone calls her “she” but gets excited if someone calls her “he.”

The non-binary person prefers not to characterize themselves as girls or boys. However, the 19-year-old is also okay if people use “they” when referring to her.

More About Bella Ramsey

Bella Ramsey was born Isabella May Ramsey in Nottingham, England, on September 30, 2003. 

She studied online through InterHigh School and started acting as a hobby at four through the Stagecoach Theatre Arts’ Loughborough branch. 

She studied acting for seven years before attending the Television Workshop and auditioning for professional roles.

Ramsey made her major credited acting debut as Lyanna Mormont in the HBO fantasy series Game of Thrones.

Fans and critics praised Bella’s portrayal of the no-nonsense Mormont in her debut appearance in S6E7, “The Broken Man.”

The Hollywood Reporter called the English actress the breakout star of Season 6. She continued her role in the seventh and eighth seasons.

She portrayed the titular character in the 2022 medieval comedy movie Catherine Called Birdy. 

Ramsey has also been credited for lending her voice as Hilda to the Netflix animated series Hilda. 

The English actress has received critical acclaim for her roles in movies and series. She has been nominated for several awards, winning a few of them. 

The 20 years old is cast as the teenager Ellie in the new series, The Last of Us, alongside GOT alumni Pedro Pascal, who portrays Joel.

The first episode was released on HBO on January 15, 2023, and received universal acclaim, with many calling it the best cinematic adaptation of a video game.

Bella Ramsey Perfected American Accent By Swearing

According to Indie Wire, Bella Ramsey improved her American accent by swearing. The news spread like wildfire after she made an appearance on The Late Late Show with James Corden.

During Bella’s time there, she addressed that she learned to swear in an American accent to give life to her post-apocalyptic video game character Ellie.

“I had a dialect coach who was helpful.” Further, she adds, “The first phrase I mastered was ‘olive oil.’ It’s really hard, like, ‘olive oil.'”

After that, Bella shared that the next word she learned was a curse word bleeped out on the broadcast. Furthermore, she said;

“Yeah, because the character that I play in this show, ‘The Last of Us,’ Ellie, curses every two seconds. So that was definitely important. There are so many [curse words].”
